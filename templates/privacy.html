Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and enable filtering. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for your benefit.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the app.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This may result in your browser making requests to Google's servers. We do not share your expense data or account information with any third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We will respond as promptly as possible.
